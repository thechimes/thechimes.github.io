Funnel Cake

From the kitchen of Phyllis Himes, revised by Calee Cecconi

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • ½ teaspoon of soda
  • ¾ tablespoon of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ⅔ cup of milk

Optional Ingredients

  • ¼ cup of powdered sugar

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together dry ingredients.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients in a small bowl.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat one inch of oil to 350° F in a large pan.
  5. Pour batter through a funnel into the oil.
  6. Fry for 2-3 minutes until golden, flip using tongs.
  7. Fry for 2-3 more minutes.
  8. Remove from oil and plate.
  9. Dust with powdered sugar.

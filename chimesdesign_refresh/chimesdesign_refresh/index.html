hello.

My name is Calee. I’m a designer, multimedia artist, educator, and aspiring bass player. I work and play in the fields I love most: art and design—the blurred line between those worlds is where I thrive. I'm passionate about design for good and social justice. Good design is more than just aesthetics; it has the power to transform lives.

I currently split my time between my full-time art and design practice and my graphic design teaching positions. In my spare time I design surface patterns, make art and home goods, craft, listen to and play music, write, spend time outdoors, and make delicious food.
